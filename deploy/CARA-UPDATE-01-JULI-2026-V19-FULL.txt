3. JANGAN upload public/.user.ini — menyebabkan error 500 di hosting ini

=== LANGKAH UPLOAD ===

1. Login DirectAdmin → File Manager

2. Masuk folder ROOT:
   /home/damgocom/domains/chatbot-keperawatan.damgocompany.com/
   (tempat file artisan)

3. Upload ZIP:
   chatbot-keperawatan-full-01juli2026-v19.zip

4. Extract → OVERWRITE semua

5. Pastikan .env masih ada:
   APP_URL=https://chatbot-keperawatan.damgocompany.com
   APP_TIMEZONE=Asia/Jakarta
   TTS_DRIVER=php
   VIDEO_MAX_UPLOAD_KB=122880

6. HAPUS jika muncul setelah extract:
   - public/.user.ini  (WAJIB — bikin error 500)
   - public/hot
   - public_html/hot

7. PHP Settings (DirectAdmin panel, BUKAN .user.ini):
   upload_max_filesize = 128M
   post_max_size = 128M
   max_execution_time = 300
   max_input_time = 300
   memory_limit = 256M

8. Symlink storage (jika belum):
   public/storage → ../storage/app/public

9. Upload deploy/fix-500.php ke public/ (sementara), buka:
   https://chatbot-keperawatan.damgocompany.com/fix-500.php?key=ck2026fix

10. Upload deploy/clear-cache.php ke public/, buka:
    https://chatbot-keperawatan.damgocompany.com/clear-cache.php?key=ck2026fix

11. Hapus fix-500.php, check.php, clear-cache.php dari public/
